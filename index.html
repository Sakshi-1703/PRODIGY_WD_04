Projects

Floor Cleaning Bot

In the context of a mini-project, I spearheaded the development of a Bluetooth-operated floor cleaning bot. This innovative creation seamlessly integrated cleaning functionalities with precise movement capabilities, enabling it to navigate forward, backward, and sideways in response to user commands transmitted via Bluetooth. The bot's core operations were facilitated by an Arduino microcontroller, ensuring efficient control and responsiveness to user inputs.

Cansat

In an ambitious project, I successfully developed a CANSAT replica with my team, that seamlessly integrated four essential sensors: GPS, MPU6050, BME280, and MQ-07. This cutting-edge system was designed to wirelessly transmit the collected data via an NRF module, enabling real-time monitoring and analysis. The model was meticulously engineered to withstand controlled drops from significant heights, incorporating a robust dropping mechanism and a parachute system to ensure safe landing.

Smart Doorbell

A smart Doorbell that captures live images of the visitor and sends the image to the owner through telegram app and allows the owner to unlock/lock the door from remote area(inside the house). I have specifically worked for the telegram bot part of the project

Precision Irrigation Systems

As part of the Precision Irrigation System project, I played a crucial role in developing the web interface. Leveraging my expertise in web development, I created an intuitive platform that analyzes sensor readings and automates water scheduling for crops based on the data analysis. This innovative solution streamlines the irrigation process, ensuring optimal resource utilization and enhancing agricultural efficiency.
